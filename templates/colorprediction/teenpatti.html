Color Prediction is a simple and exciting game where you predict the outcome of each round. Each round lasts 30 seconds, and you can place your bet anytime before the round ends.

You can bet on colors, numbers, or odd/even outcomes. Each bet type has a different payout multiplier.

After placing your bet, wait for the round to end and see if your prediction matches the result!

In the Colors mode, you bet on one of three colors: Red, Green, or Blue.

If the result matches your color, you win 3 times your bet amount.

Colors are associated with numbers: Red (1, 4, 7), Green (2, 5, 8), Blue (0, 3, 6, 9).

In the Numbers mode, you bet on a specific number from 0 to 9.

If the result matches your number, you win 9 times your bet amount.

This mode offers the highest potential payout but is the hardest to win.

In the Odd/Even mode, you bet on whether the resulting number will be odd or even.

Odd numbers are 1, 3, 5, 7, 9. Even numbers are 0, 2, 4, 6, 8.

If your prediction is correct, you win 2 times your bet amount.

This mode has the highest chances of winning but with a lower payout.
